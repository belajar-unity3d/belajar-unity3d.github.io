Menyiapkan Analytics

  1. Buka jendela Services (Window > General > Services), dan klik Analytics.
  2. Pada bagian Analytics dari menu Project SettingsA broad collection of settings which allow you to configure how Physics, Audio, Networking, Graphics, Input and many other areas of your project behave. More info
